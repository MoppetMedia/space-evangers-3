Liz Elwood

Liz Elwood is a missionary worker at United Fellowship Organization (UFO). She is an expert linguist, translator, interpreter, and teacher. Her missions often involve church planting and teaching the gospel to alien children. She was married to the missionary martyr Jon Elwood and her most valuable possessions are his diaries, journals, and letters to her.

Liz Elwood is based on the real-life missionary Elisabeth Elliot.
